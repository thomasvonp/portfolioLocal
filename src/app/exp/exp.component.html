About me

My name is Thomas von Prouss and I live in Stockholm, Sweden. I am currently looking for work within my field of study. I started studying Media Technology in 2013 while working part time at Elgiganten. The choice for this programme was made based on how incredibly tech-related it is. A big plus was also how broad the courses were, hence they allowed me to work with a variety of projects hands-on.
What I learned the most from this programme is how to start, maintain and complete a project regardless of how it begins. Everything from UX-design to back-end has been incredibly exciting to work with simply due to the potential of today's technologies. Two of the field trips I've had the pleasure of doing during my studies was to the HQ of King and Spotify in Stockholm. Two widely different employers, yet both simply astounding with rich culture.
