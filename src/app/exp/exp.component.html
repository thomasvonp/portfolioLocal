About me

Background My name is Thomas von Prouss and I live in Stockholm, Sweden. I am currently looking for work within my field of study. While studying, I have been working part time at Elgiganten. The reason for moving from Aftersales to Sales in 2017 was to seek new challenges and widen my expertise. Learning something new is an exciting part of working, especially considering how different the approach towards customers is between these departments. On one hand, there is a lot of mailing, phone contact and working with a monitor, while on the other is body language, rhetorics and needs assessment on the spot.

I started studying Media Technology in 2013. Choosing Media Technology was based on how incredibly tech-related it is. A big plus was also how broad the courses were, hence they allowed me to work with a variety of projects hands-on. What I learned the most from this programme is how to start, maintain and complete a project regardless of how it begins. Everything from UX-design to back-end has been incredibly exciting to work with simply due to the potential of today's technologies. Two of the field trips I've had the pleasure of doing during my studies was to the HQ of King and Spotify in Stockholm. Two widely different employers, yet both had a very inspiring work culture.
